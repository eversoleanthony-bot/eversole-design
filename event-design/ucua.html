UCUA Credit Union Summit 2026

The Utah Credit Union Association's annual summit, drawing attendees from across Utah for two days of education, networking, and industry programming at the Hyatt Regency Salt Lake City. Design included banners, SEG graphics, and motion graphics for LED displays, built as a system meant to carry the summit through its next few years of programming.
